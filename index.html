How unreported assets are measured

For each household, we compare the number of asset types marked as owned in the main questionnaire (own_asset__* = yes) with the number of distinct asset types recorded in asset_details_roster with quantity greater than zero. The unreported asset gap is the shortfall (zero when the roster matches reported ownership). Use the threshold below to list households where the gap is at or above your chosen level.

Rules (reference)

Urban/rural is taken from the sample frame (targets.json, EA urban_rural). Assets = distinct asset types in asset_details_roster with quantity > 0. Consumption (7-day recall) = number of unique food items consumed in the last 7 days from the Food Recall section (food_recall_details); quantity does not matter (e.g. 1 tomato or 25 tomatoes counts as one item).
